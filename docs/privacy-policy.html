Privacy Policy

Top Tipster Racing · Last updated: 2025

This policy describes how we collect, use and protect your information when you use the Top Tipster Racing app.

Information we collect

  • Account information: Email address and a username (for the leaderboard) when you sign up.
  • Selections and competition data: Your race selections, competition entries and display names you provide when joining competitions.
  • Device: We may use standard technologies (e.g. for push notifications or app stability) that do not personally identify you.

How we use it

We use your data to run the app: to create and manage your account, show your name on leaderboards, store your selections and results, and send you optional reminders (e.g. before selections close). We do not sell your data to third parties.

Storage and security

Your data is stored and processed using Supabase (hosted infrastructure). We use industry-standard measures to keep it secure. Data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Your rights

You can delete your account at any time from within the app (Account → Delete account). This permanently removes your account and associated data. You can also sign out without deleting.

Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, contact us using the details provided in the app or on the app store listing.
